About Us

Lefarm.com was inspired by my personal journey of connecting local farmers with customers seeking fresh, high-quality produce. Growing up in a farming community, I witnessed the challenges farmers face in getting their products to market. This project is a way to bridge that gap and provide a platform that benefits both farmers and customers. The project timeline spans over six months, involving research, development, and testing phases.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
